Takeaways from AR/VR VAP Session

Understanding AR & VR

AR (Augmented Reality) overlays digital information onto the real world, while VR (Virtual Reality) creates a fully immersive simulated environment.

Types of AR

Marker-Based AR

  • Uses QR codes or predefined patterns to trigger digital content.
  • Examples: AR-enabled books, toys, tattoos, and mobile skins.

Markerless AR

  • Uses SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) to detect surroundings.
  • Examples: Pok√©mon GO, holograms, and real-world overlays.

How to Experience AR

  • Web-Based AR: Access AR via a browser (WebAR).
  • Mobile Apps: Apps like IKEA Place visualize objects in real-world spaces.
  • Social Media AR: Platforms like SPARK AR (Meta), SNAP AR (Snapchat), and Effect House (TikTok) allow AR filter creation.

VR Technologies

  • Meta Quest 2 & 3: Standalone VR headsets for immersive experiences.
  • Meta Horizon: Social VR platform by Meta.
  • SideQuest: Allows installing and streaming custom VR apps on Meta Quest.
